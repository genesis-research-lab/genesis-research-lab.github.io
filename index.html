Welcome to our lab

At Genesis, we are creating the first global cryptocurrency and blockchain research lab connecting the United Kingdom, Europe, and the United Arab Emirates. We believe that a research lab should not be just an individual center but a network which unites researchers globally. Our core mission is to develop and transfer the necessary methodologies, tools, and technologies to advance the research in the field of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology, and bridge the gap between academics, practitioners and enthusiasts.
